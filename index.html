About PREASX24

Seakwa Jonas Mochebane, widely known as PREASX24, is a South African web developer and project manager focused on building practical, accessible digital systems.

He is the founder of DTECH Services, a growing digital ecosystem created to empower students and the younger generation through free access to modern technology.

At its core, DTECH is an ecosystem of tools built for the general public — with a strong focus on students and the younger generation. The work goes beyond building websites; it involves engineering practical digital systems that combine multiple technologies to solve real-world challenges.

Many of the functions these systems perform would typically exist behind paid platforms or subscription-based services. Through smart integration, automation, and efficient use of available technology, DTECH restructures those processes and makes them freely accessible.

The broader objective is digital equality — ensuring that access to modern tools, platforms, and technological infrastructure is not determined by income, but available to anyone growing within today’s digital era.
