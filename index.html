About PREASX24

Seakwa Jonas Mochebane, widely known as PREASX24, is a South African web developer and project manager based in Limpopo. He specializes in creating practical digital ecosystems designed to empower students and local communities.

As the visionary behind DTECH Services, Jonas has built a network of platforms that bridge the gap between education and technology in South Africa, providing essential resources for high school and university students.
